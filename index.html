The Graduate Climate Conference (GCC) is an interdisciplinary climate conference run by graduate students, for graduate students. Over the last nineteen years, graduate students representing hundreds of academic institutions have come together to present research and share ideas on climate and climate change in an array of disciplines. We welcome abstracts from diverse fields such as atmospheric sciences, biology, environmental management, forestry and fisheries sciences, oceanography, communication, public policy studies, urban planning, public health, and any other climate-related disciplines.

The 19th Annual GCC will take place on November 7-9, 2025 at the Marine Biological Laboratory, located in Woods Hole, Massachusetts.

We will begin accepting applications for GCC 2025 soon!
