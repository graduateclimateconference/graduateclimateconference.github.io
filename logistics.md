# Location

GCC 2024 will be on November 1 - 3, 2024 at the [Pack Forest Conference Center](https://www.packforest.org/index.html){:target="_blank"}, Eatonville, Washington. Pack Forest is 1.5 hours south of the University of Washington campus in Seattle and offers shared cabins as accomodations. Transportation between UW and Pack Forest will be provided. Please email [gradclimateconference@gmail.com](mailto:gradclimateconference@gmail.com) for any inquries. Thank you!
